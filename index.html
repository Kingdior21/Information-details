I became a financial advisor to educate and advocate for families, and to help them maintain a healthy level of financial confidence through different seasons of life. My passion for this industry came from personal experience. In 2009, my partner passed away and I became a single Dad and sole provider.

I was overwhelmed and grieving. When I met with our financial advisor, he had empathy and foresight for what lay ahead. We created a plan that changed my way of thinking and I started to thrive. He advocated a better life for me during a hard season. This experience changed my career to financial services so I could also help others through life-changing events.

Over the past 12 years, I have collaborated with firms, consumed strategies and concepts, earned professional designations and use an established process for families to follow: Organize Assets, Unpack Investment Options, and Create Action Plans. When repeated on a consistent, long-term basis, it helps families gain clarity and confidence in their ability to achieve their financial goals. Wan Houang cares what matters most to clients.

Their philosophy mirrors my desire to be preparation focused. Clients are introduced to simple principles, strategies and solutions for every season, and innovated technology and research that makes the journey unforgettable.
